⚙️ Stop the Leak: Modular "Retention Engine" for Service Businesses

In 10 years of finance, I’ve learned that the most expensive customer is the one you already have but forget to follow up with. Whether it’s a car service, a dental check-up, or a road tax renewal, businesses lose thousands in "leaky" revenue simply because life gets busy and customers forget. I’ve developed a systematic solution using n8n to automate this entire lifecycle.

The "Universal Reminder" Framework

Instead of a simple "one-off" email, I built a 3-tier "Waterfall" system:

  • 30 Days Out: Awareness & Planning.
  • 14 Days Out: Active Scheduling.
  • 7 Days Out: The Urgent "Final Call."

  • ❄️ Air-Con Servicing: Ensuring homes stay cool in our Malaysian heat before the unit breaks down.

  • 📋 Insurance & Road Tax: Critical 30-day countdowns to keep drivers legal and covered.
  • 📑 Accounting/Secretarial: Managing non-negotiable filing deadlines for SSM and LHDN.

The Best Part? It’s Modular.

While the current engine sends professional Emails, the "Brain" is channel-agnostic. Swapping to WhatsApp Business is a simple plug-and-play upgrade without rewriting the core logic.

Automation is about more than just speed—it’s about creating a predictable, reliable customer experience.

⚙️ Stop Manual Posting: Social Media Distribution Engine

Diving deep into n8n and API automation to solve a common business headache: the "Social Media Time Sink." Instead of manual uploads, I’ve built a self-hosted automation engine on Zeabur that manages content distribution from a single Google Sheets command center.

The Workflow Breakdown

  • ✅ Central Database: Google Sheets acts as the "Brain" where I schedule content, image URLs, and target platforms.
  • ✅ Intelligent Filtering: The system checks the clock and only picks up "Pending" posts that are due.
  • ✅ Binary Image Handling: It doesn't just send links; the workflow fetches raw image data from Google Drive to ensure high-quality native uploads.
  • ✅ Multi-Platform Routing: Using conditional logic, it routes content to LinkedIn and Facebook simultaneously.
  • ✅ Closed-Loop Logic: Once posted, n8n writes back to the sheet to update the status to "Posted"—preventing duplicates and maintaining a perfect audit trail.

Business Use Cases & Potential

  • 🔹 Small Businesses (SMEs): Perfect for brands repo, allowing owners to focus on production while the "Digital Assistant" handles the marketing.
  • 🔹 Lean Marketing Teams: One person can manage 5+ platforms without ever leaving a spreadsheet.
  • 🔹 Finance & Compliance: In my 10 years in finance, I’ve seen how manual errors creep in. This system creates a clear, automated record of what was communicated and when.
  • 🔹 Scalability: This isn't just for social media. This same logic can be applied to automated invoicing, reporting, or CRM updates.

Automation isn't about replacing the human element; it's about removing the "robotic" tasks from the human workday.

⚙️ AI-Driven News Intelligence Engine

In 2026, the problem isn't a lack of information—it’s the "noise" that comes with it. I spent the last few days perfecting an AI-Driven News Intelligence Engine that transforms a chaotic RSS feed into a high-value daily briefing.

The Architecture: Built for Efficiency

  • ✅ The Watcher (Real-time): An RSS Feed Trigger monitors sources like Techmeme 24/7. It only "wakes up" when a brand-new story breaks.
  • ✅ The Analyst (AI): Instead of reading long snippets, the DeepSeek API generates a punchy, one-sentence summary for every article.
  • ✅ The Archive (Database): Data is structured in a Google Sheet—complete with secure HTTPS image extraction and deduplication logic.
  • ✅ The Delivery (Automated): Once a day, the system assembles the "unsent" news into a professional, mobile-responsive HTML newsletter and delivers it straight to my inbox.

The 3 Key Benefits

  • 🔹 Zero Signal-to-Noise Ratio: I don't see ads, sponsored "fluff," or duplicate headlines. I only see the core facts.
  • 🔹 Infrastructure Ownership: Unlike paid newsletter services, I own the data and the logic. There are no monthly subscriptions, only minimal API costs.
  • 🔹 Scalability: This system can handle 10 articles or 1,000 without me lifting a finger.

Beyond Personal Use: Business Use Cases 💼

  • 📈 Competitor Intelligence: Automatically track every mention of a competitor’s product or stock price and summarize the sentiment for your executive team.
  • 📈 Sales Enablement: Feed your sales team a daily "Morning Brief" of their top 50 prospects' company news, so they always have a warm talking point for calls.
  • 📈 Internal Knowledge Management: Keep your engineering or marketing teams updated on industry shifts without forcing them to spend hours on social media or news sites.
  • 📈 PR & Brand Monitoring: Track brand mentions across the web and instantly get an AI-generated "Urgency Score" for each mention.

🚀 Upgrade: Supercharging AI & Finance Insights

  • 4️⃣ Time-Saving Automation: Delivered directly via newsletter & Telegram — no endless scrolling.
  • 5️⃣ Actionable Insights: Not just headlines — each update gives context for decision-making.
